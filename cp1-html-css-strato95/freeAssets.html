While paid assets are often the highest quality that can be used in a game or application, free assets are often
sufficient in many circumstances. Often, these are objects or other small components of a scene, rather than major
components such as the environment, or particle effects.

The Stylized Crystal is a colorful asset that
can easily be added to a mountain or mine scene.

The Low Polygon Potions are great to use as shop items.

The Lion Statue has a great base that can be used to form
other animal statues as well.

The Hand Painted Fountain has a large number of polygons, but
it can be used for old-fashioned scenes with ease.

The Low Poly Trashbin is a standard, small trash
can that can make a city look more realistic.

Skybox: This asset is a red, twighlight skybox that
looks good in many scenes.

The Snowed Fence is a great alternative in if it is
not possible to use Enviro (a paid asset) for weather

Temple Props are hexagonal, small towers that
can easily be used to create a temple with columns.

The Victorian PBR Streetpghts look a bit old but
can be used for either day or night scenes.

Note: All images of assets are from the Unity Asset Store
and all links to assets direct to the asset page on the unity asset store.
