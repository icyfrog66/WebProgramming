Because it can be cumbersome to make materials from scratch in Unity, it is often preferable to
use assets from the Unity Asset Store. Many of these assets are free, and often these are low-poly
assets that can fit in mobile applications. However, the highest quality assets are often not free.

Tips for the Assets

Do not download too many: it will take up too much space.

Do not download assets that are not easily compatible with new versions of unity.
Most likely you will need to delete those assets anyways due to incompatilbility.

Check all assets for prefabs, in case you want to keep the original version.

Note: All images of assets are from the Unity Asset Store
and all links to assets direct to the asset page on the unity asset store.
